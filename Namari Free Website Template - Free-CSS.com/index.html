BIZQUEST'24

BizQuest is a dynamic business plan competition organized by Thapar Venture Club, designed to challenge participants to develop innovative, actionable solutions to real-world business problems. The competition provides a unique platform for aspiring entrepreneurs, ideators, and emerging startups to showcase their skills and ideas.

Event Details

The competition consists of three distinct tracks, to include everyone from experienced startup owners to those new to their entrepreneurial journey. Each team must submit an initial pitch deck as per the given guide.

Track 1 : Ideators (Participants without an idea)

Teams will be provided with problem statements and must submit a pitch deck that includes a proposed solution with basic market research and a potential business model.

Track 2 : Aspiring Entrepreneurs (Participants with an idea)

Teams with existing ideas must submit a pitch deck that includes a basic business model outlining revenue generation, market demand and initial validation(if available).

Track 3 : Emerging Startups (Participants with MVP)

Teams with a working MVP must submit a pitch deck that includes its overview and the problem it solves, customer feedback, scalability potential and next-stage funding needs.

Upon successful registration, each team will participate in an online preliminary quiz designed to test their foundational business knowledge. This elimination round will determine which teams advance to compete on the final day before a panel of judges. Only the passing teams will move forward, ensuring a highly competitive atmosphere. On the event day, November 16, 2024, teams will compete in three tailored rounds within their respective tracks. These rounds will challenge their skills and strategies, ultimately determining the winners in each category.

Round 1

All teams submit their initial pitch decks for evaluation by a panel of judges, who will assess on the basis of creativity of solution, market research, and the clarity of the business model. Teams with the highest scores will advance to the next round.

Round 2

Teams will further develop and refine their solutions or business ideas focusing on- desirability, feasibility and viability. They must submit a detailed analysis through customer research, feasibility studies, or financial projections.

Round 3

Teams will deliver a 10-minute pitch, followed by a 5-minute Q&A session with the judges. The presentation must highlight problem and solution overview, detailed business model, market validation, financial forecasts and growth plans.

BizQuest is not just a competition—it's a platform to test your ideas, refine your strategies, and gain the skills needed to turn your vision into reality. Whether scaling a startup, pitching an idea, or solving a business challenge, BizQuest offers the resources and mentorship to help you succeed.

About TVC

Thapar Venture Club (previously Entrepreneurship Development Cell), TIET, Patiala, is a vibrant community of innovators and dreamers, dedicated to turning ideas into impactful ventures. At TVC, some of the brightest minds on campus come together to create, collaborate, and support one another’s entrepreneurial journeys. We believe that every great business starts with a spark, and our mission is to nurture that spark into a thriving startup.

TVC empowers students by making resources accessible and connecting them with industry leaders, venture capitalists, and successful entrepreneurs. Through mentorship, practical guidance, and hands-on opportunities, we equip aspiring founders to navigate the challenges of building a business. With a keen eye on the dynamic Indian startup landscape, TVC is committed to helping every member unlock their potential and watch their entrepreneurial dreams take flight.

Join us, and become part of a legacy where ideas flourish, ventures thrive, and the entrepreneurs of tomorrow are born.

About TIET

Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology (TIET), founded in 1956, stands as a symbol of academic excellence and innovation. Spread across 250 acres, TIET is more than just an educational institution; it is a thriving community of problem solvers and critical thinkers, dedicated to creating a positive impact on the world. With an ‘A’ grade accreditation from NAAC, we are a highly ranked, research-driven university that nurtures talent in engineering, innovation, and research. Our state-of-the-art infrastructure and progressive teaching methodologies ensure the development of well-rounded individuals with diverse skills, ready to lead in the world of tomorrow.
